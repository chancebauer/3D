Have you ever wondered why we exist? Or why things are the way they are? I think everyone has, but most people put those thoughts to the back of their mind and go about their day. Well, I believe life isn't at all what we think. I understand we have a fundamental basis of how we have come about to exist on this planet, but the true reality is we as humans have a very limited understanding of our existence. The simple fact is that the smartest people utilizing the most powerful computers and software do not know what is truly out there in the universe. That really blows my mind.

I created this short page to remind myself that life is very valuable and so important. We must not overthink the miniscule of things. We are far from the peak understanding of our existence. In a sense everything and everyone that has ever existed and currently exists is going through this moment in time. We are constantly moving very fast through space stuck on this huge ball of matter we call earth. Wherever we end up we must constantly question the motives and understand there is so much more out there than we realize.
